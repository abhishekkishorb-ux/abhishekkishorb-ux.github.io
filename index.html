About

I'm an electrical and electronics engineer with a focus on motor design and energy efficiency, currently working through problems where electromagnetics, thermal analysis, and real-world manufacturing standards meet. My final-year project redesigned 1.1kW–30kW induction motors to meet IE3, IE4, and IE5 efficiency classes, work that later became a published paper in the IEEE Canadian Journal of Electrical and Computer Engineering.

Alongside motor design, I've built IoT and embedded systems — from a solar-integrated EV range study to a smart sewage monitoring framework for sustainable homes. I like projects that sit at the intersection of hardware, simulation, and measurable efficiency gains.
